Introduction

I'm Giacomo, a visual and digital designer from Turin, Italy. At the end of 2009 I graduated in Graphic and Virtual Design at the Politecnico di Torino. A few months later I moved to Berlin, where I am currently living and working as Head of Design at Remerge and freelancing sometimes. I like coding, human interactions, visual communication and music. I always try to mix all of these passions in my projects in a digital or analogue way.
